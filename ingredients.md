TAWA PIZZA RECIPE
Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 15 minutes
Serves: 4

Ingredients:
For Tawa Pizza dough:
1 cup All purpose flour 
¼ cup Wheat flour
1/2 tsp Baking powder
1/2 tsp Baking soda 
1 tbsp Oil
Salt to taste 
3 tbsp Curd        

For Topping
2 tbsp Olive oil
2 Capcium 
1 Onion
3-4 Mushroom
1 Tomato
Salt to taste 
1 tsp Chilli flakes
1 tsp Oregano 
4-5 Basil leaves

For Sauce
1 tbsp Oil
2-3 Garlic
1 medium Onion
½ cup fresh Tomato Puree
½ tsp Sugar
½ tsp Vinegar
Salt to taste
½ tsp Oregano 
½ tsp Chilli flakes
⅓ cup Mozzarella cheese or Pizza cheese
